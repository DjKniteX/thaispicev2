Our Story

Welcome

Thai Spice is a Thai resturant based in Fayetteville, Arkansas in the heart of NWA. Among the many different resturants available in the area, Thai Spice will always cook to your standard with our family-style dishes and cooks who have been cooking for their entire lifetime.

Our foundations is built on culture and family. Thai Spice was founded by a family who have immigrated here from Asia and now wants to share their food and culture with the residents as well as tourists who are visiting Arkansas. Visit today and enjoy the taste of Thai food.
